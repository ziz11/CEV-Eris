17 May 2020

Carbonhell updated:

  • Refactored the perk system to be easier to use and more robust.
  • Perks now show up with their icon, name and description in the "Show stats and perks" in IC tab.
  • All IH members start with a perk that reduces their sanity loss at the sight of the dead.
  • All Moebius staff start with a perk that reduces the chance to trigger a chem addiction, and boosts basic NSA by 10.
  • All Vagabonds start with a perk that caps their environmental sanity loss to 40 instead of the default 20.
  • Guild Merchants start with a perk that lets them use cash bundles as inspiration objects, instead of just oddities. More means more levels, and the stat is picked randomly.
  • All the guild workers start with a perk that gives them one of four possible benefits: a random language, knowledge of an active traitor contract, a stash location paper or a rare object.
  • All NeoTheology crew starts with a perk that boosts their sanity regeneration when near an altar.
